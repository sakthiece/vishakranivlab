Reaction Timer - NOT gate and its application in Fuel Level Indicator

Procedure




Step-1) Move the slider upwards. This condition simulates that the fuel level is sufficient.

Step-2) Observe the LED output.

Step-3) Move the slider downwards, below the minimum/reserve marking.

Step-4) Observe the output LED. Check whether the drop in fuel level is indicated by the glowing of the RED LED.

Step-5) Build and verify the same circuit using simulator.
